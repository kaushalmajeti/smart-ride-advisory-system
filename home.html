AI ride comparison and recommendation system

Smart Ride

A machine-learning ride assistant that compares fares, predicts surge, understands customer type, and recommends the most suitable Ola or Uber ride.

Open App
What it does

One interface for route, fare, history, and AI recommendation.

01

Ride Price Comparison

Calculates route distance and displays available fare options across Ola Auto, Ola Mini, Uber Go, and Uber Sedan.

02

Customer Segmentation

Uses K-Means style customer profiling to classify riders as Budget Rider, Premium Commuter, Weekend Explorer, Night Owl, or Regular Rider.

03

Personal AI Recommendation

Combines route fit, time context, ride history, and customer segment to recommend the best ride for that user.

04

Model Dashboard

Shows model metrics, confusion matrices, surge model quality, and clustering output for project demonstration.

ML pipeline

Built around a full ensemble model stack.

Smart Ride uses XGBoost, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting as base learners in a stacking ensemble. LSTM adds ride-history sequence behavior, K-Means creates customer segments, and surge regression estimates dynamic pricing.

How recommendation happens

From pickup and drop to final ride choice.

1

Enter Route

The app geocodes pickup and drop locations, then calculates route distance.

2

Generate Fares

Fare logic applies distance pricing and surge multiplier to every available ride.

3

Profile Customer

History and K-Means segmentation identify budget, premium, weekend, or night behavior.

4

Recommend Ride

The final score combines ML probabilities with the user segment bias.
